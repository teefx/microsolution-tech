About MicroSolutions Technology Limited

MicroSolutions Technology Limited, a sister company of InfoByte Technologies, specializes in IT solutions tailored for private and non-governmental organizations. With a focus on guiding clients from manual processes to fully computerized systems, MicroSolutions ensures seamless transitions that enhance operational efficiency. With an experienced team, MicroSolutions has successfully implemented numerous projects across diverse sectors, demonstrating expertise in digital transformation, system integration, and software solutions. This commitment to delivering high-quality service has solidified its reputation as a trusted partner for innovative IT advancements in Nigeria.

Corporate Status

Our Operational Groups

MicroSolutions Consultancy (MSConsult)
